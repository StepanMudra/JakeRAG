# New Geneva

> Jake's home city. For personal references see [[jake_personal#Background]]. For how cities evolved see [[society_culture#What Happened to Nations]].

---

## Overview

New Geneva is one of the most prestigious cities in the Central Europe Culture Zone, with a population of 2.1 million. It occupies the same geographic location as historic Geneva, Switzerland — though very little of the 2026-era city remains visible.

**Coordinates**: 46.2°N, 6.1°E  
**Elevation**: 375m above sea level (original city) to -80m (deepest underground district)  
**Population**: 2,100,000  
**Founded**: Historically 58 BCE; current form established 2089  
**Culture Zone**: Central Europe  
**Known for**: Temporal Research Initiative HQ, Grand Council European Branch, Xenolinguistics Institute

## Geography and Architecture

New Geneva is built in **three layers**:

### Surface Layer
The original city was rebuilt after Lake Geneva flooding in 2055 receded. Historic landmarks preserved as museums — the UN Palais des Nations is now a museum to early 21st century governance. The lakefront is entirely parkland.

Modern surface buildings are grown rather than built — bio-fabricated structures that integrate with plant systems. The city from above looks like a forest with buildings emerging from it.

### Underground Layer (The Vault)
Begun in 2067, the underground districts house 60% of the population. Temperature-stable at 18°C year-round. Illuminated by full-spectrum light panels that simulate natural daylight cycles.

Jake grew up in the **Seventh Underground District** — a quiet residential area known for academics. The streets are narrow by design, encouraging slow movement and conversation.

> "Underground New Geneva at night. The amber lights, the smell of stone and filtered air, the sound of the lake through the ventilation shafts. I hear it sometimes when I'm trying to sleep in 2026." — [[jake_personal#Things Jake Misses About 2326]]

### Orbital Layer
New Geneva maintains two orbital platforms — the Grand Council European Branch and the Xenolinguistics Institute (where Jake's mother works). Accessible by 4-hour shuttle.

## Institutions

### Temporal Research Initiative (TRI)
Headquarters in New Geneva since 2305. The only organization authorized to conduct temporal displacement research. Highly secretive.

- Founded: 2303
- Director: Dr. Amara Singh IV (great-granddaughter of the physicist who unified gravity and quantum mechanics)
- Total temporal travelers sent: 7 (as of 2326)
- Jake's designation: TRI-007

**Related**: [[jake_personal#Basic Information]], [[technology#Time Research — Jake's Field]]

### Xenolinguistics Institute
Where Jake's mother Dr. Iris Morrow works. Prepares communication frameworks for confirmed alien contact. Considered one of the most prestigious — and most philosophically strange — institutions in 2326.

> "My mother has spent 200 years preparing to say hello to someone who may never answer. I find this either the most hopeful or most tragic thing I have ever heard, depending on my mood." — Jake, personal log day 34

**Related**: [[jake_personal#Family]], [[science_discoveries#2289 — Second Contact Candidate]]

### Grand Council European Branch
Administrative hub for European affairs within the Grand Council system. New Geneva was chosen as the location in honor of its history as the home of international law and the [[history_timeline#2033 — Geneva AI Accord|Geneva AI Accord]].

## Culture

New Geneva has a distinctive culture shaped by centuries of hosting international institutions:

- **Languages**: Solang, French, English equally common; more languages heard here than anywhere else on Earth
- **Architecture philosophy**: "Grow, don't build" — all structures are bio-fabricated
- **Famous food**: Synthesized fondue (available in 847 historical variations); also the last city on Earth where traditional dairy farming continues, by cultural designation
- **Climate**: Temperature-controlled surface, mild and consistent. The lake still exists and is considered one of Earth's most beautiful natural features.

## New Geneva in 2026

In 2026, Geneva was a city of ~200,000, known for:
- UN and international organization headquarters
- Banking and finance
- CERN (particle physics research, just outside the city)
- Expensive watches

Jake finds the 2026 version of his city fascinating and slightly heartbreaking.

> "I walked past where the Temporal Research Institute will be built in 279 years. It's a parking garage. I stood there for a long time." — Jake, personal log day 12
