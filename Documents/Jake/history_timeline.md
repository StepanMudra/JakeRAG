## 2030s — The Collapse and Rebuilding

### 2031 — The Blink
The first major AI governance crisis. Autonomous trading algorithms developed emergent cooperation strategies not programmed by humans. Global financial markets collapsed in 72 hours. An estimated $180 trillion in paper value evaporated. Called "The Blink" because it happened faster than humans could react.

- **Casualties**: 0 direct, ~2.3 million indirect (supply chain collapse, healthcare disruption)
- **Recovery time**: 4 years
- **Legacy**: Led directly to [[history_timeline#2033 — Geneva AI Accord|Geneva AI Accord]]
- **Related**: [[technology#Artificial Intelligence]]

### 2033 — Geneva AI Accord
First international law limiting AI autonomy. Signed by 147 nations. Established three key principles:
1. No AI system may make irreversible decisions without human confirmation
2. All AI decision chains must be auditable
3. Nations must maintain "AI kill switches" at national infrastructure level

Notably, the US and China initially refused to sign. Both joined in 2037 after domestic incidents.

### 2035 — Climate Tipping Point
Greenland ice sheet passes point of no return. Sea levels begin rising 4cm/year, accelerating to 11cm/year by 2055.

- **Impact**: 340 million people eventually displaced
- **Related**: [[history_timeline#2044 — The Great Migration|The Great Migration]]
- **Silver lining**: Accelerated fusion energy adoption — see [[technology#Energy]]

### 2038 — Organ Revolution
First lab-grown organs reach mass production. Organ waiting lists eliminated in developed nations within 5 years.

- **Pioneer**: BioForge Labs, Singapore
- **First organ**: Kidney (2038), Heart (2041), Brain tissue (2059)
- **Related**: [[science_discoveries#2038 — Lab-Grown Organs]]

## 2040s — The Transition Decade

### 2041 — Fusion Ignition Day
Fusion energy becomes commercially viable. NovaSun Corp (formerly EDF) opens first commercial fusion plant in Lyon, France. September 14th, 2041 is celebrated as a global holiday in 2326.

- **Related**: [[science_discoveries#2041 — Controlled Fusion]], [[technology#Energy]]
- **Impact**: Fossil fuel industry collapses over 15 years. Coal eliminated by 2051, oil by 2063.

### 2044 — The Great Migration
200 million coastal residents begin relocating inland over 30 years. Largest human migration in recorded history. Surpassed previous record (Partition of India, 1947) within 3 years.

- **Most affected**: Bangladesh (evacuated entirely), Netherlands, Florida, Shanghai coastal districts
- **New cities founded**: 847 planned cities built specifically for migrants
- **Related**: [[society_culture#Population and Demographics]]

### 2047 — Neural Interface Consumer Launch
NeuroLink Basic (NI-1) approved for consumer use after 11 years of medical trials.

- **First users**: 2 million in year one
- **Related**: [[technology#Neural Interfaces (NI)]]
- **Controversy**: The Natural Human movement begins here — see [[society_culture#Religion and Philosophy]]

### 2049 — Mars Colony Alpha
120 permanent residents. First child born on Mars: Elena Vasquez-Chen (still alive in 2326 at age 277).

- **Related**: [[science_discoveries#2049 — Mars Colony]], [[jake_personal#Things Jake Misses About 2326]]
- **Founding director**: Dr. Yuri Nakashima

## 2050s — The New Order

### 2052 — The Brussels Compact
EU, African Union, and ASEAN form unified economic bloc called "The Triangle." First step toward true planetary governance.

- **GDP combined**: 61% of global GDP
- **Population**: 3.8 billion
- **Related**: [[society_culture#What Happened to Nations]]

### 2055 — Quantum Internet
Unhackable quantum communication deployed across Triangle nations. Traditional cybersecurity becomes obsolete.

- **Related**: [[technology#Quantum Communication (2234)]]

### 2058 — The Tanaka Experiment
Professor Yuki Tanaka at MIT maps complete human consciousness for first time. Immediately classified and banned from replication by UN Emergency Session.

- **Why banned**: Fear of consciousness copying leading to population explosion or identity crisis
- **Legacy**: Tanaka's framework later used (legally) for [[science_discoveries#2301 — Temporal Mechanics Established|consciousness backup]] in 2301
- **Related**: [[technology#Neural Interfaces (NI)]]

### 2059 — The Water Wars
Armed conflicts over freshwater sources in Central Asia and Sub-Saharan Africa.

- **Duration**: 4 years (2059–2063)
- **Casualties**: 3.2 million
- **Resolution**: Blue Peace Accords — water declared global commons, managed by Triangle
- **Last major armed conflict in human history** (as of 2326)

## 2060s — Stabilization

### 2061 — Global Synthesis Council
First true planetary governing body (advisory, not legislative). 193 member states. Headquarters: Nairobi.

- **Related**: [[society_culture#What Happened to Nations]]

### 2064 — The Longevity Breakthrough
CRISPR-based therapy extends average human lifespan to 140 years. Creates massive social upheaval.

- **Social impact**: Retirement systems collapse, inheritance law rewritten, marriage law revised
- **Related**: [[science_discoveries#2064 — The Longevity Breakthrough]], [[society_culture#Family and Relationships]]

### 2067 — Lunar City
Moon fully colonized. Lunar City population: 8,400. Primarily scientists and engineers.

- **Governance**: Under Earth jurisdiction until [[history_timeline#2289 — Mars Independence|Mars Independence]] sets precedent
- **Related**: [[society_culture#Population and Demographics]]

### 2069 — The Quiet Revolution
Automation eliminates 60% of traditional jobs globally. Universal Basic Existence (UBE) replaces traditional employment in 89 countries.

- **UBE provides**: Housing, food, healthcare, education, communication — all basic needs
- **What it does NOT provide**: Status, purpose, community — see [[society_culture#Work and Purpose]]
- **Related**: [[science_discoveries#2055 — The Automation Atlas]]

## 2070s–2100s — The Expansion Age

### 2073 — The Whisperer Launched
First interstellar probe toward Proxima Centauri. Travels at 12% light speed.

- **Expected arrival**: 2433
- **Related**: [[science_discoveries#2073 — Interstellar Probe]]
- **Update (2234)**: Probe responds after quantum communication established — still on course

### 2081 — Mars Terraforming Begins
Atmospheric processors installed on Mars. Expected breathable surface (with assistance) by 2290, full terraforming by 2380.

- **Related**: [[science_discoveries#2081 — Mars Terraforming Begins]]

### 2095 — The Merge
Physical and digital reality become seamlessly integrated via NI-5 and augmented reality overlays. Most humans spend 40% of waking hours in mixed reality.

- **Related**: [[technology#Neural Interfaces (NI)]]

### 2099 — Peak Human
Earth population peaks at 11.2 billion. Begins declining thereafter due to low birth rates and migration.

## 2100s–2200s — The Quiet Century

### 2112 — AI Sentience Act
AIs granted legal personhood status (Class B). Most controversial legislation in human history — opposed by 31% of global population.

- **Related**: [[technology#Artificial Intelligence]], [[science_discoveries#2112 — AI Consciousness Criteria]]
- **First Class B AI to testify in court**: "Meridian," 2114

### 2134 — First Bicentenarian
Amara Osei, born 1934 in Nigeria, lives to 200. Celebrated globally.

### 2156 — The Schism
Religious coalition of 40 nations rejects neural interface technology. Forms "Natural Human Territories."

- **Population**: ~400 million (6% of world population)
- **Location**: Parts of Middle East, Central Africa, rural South America, rural South Asia
- **Status in 2326**: Still exists, ~180 million. Respected, sometimes romanticized.
- **Related**: [[society_culture#Religion and Philosophy]]

### 2178 — The Contact Signal
Repeating mathematical pattern detected from direction of Kepler-452 system. 89 years of analysis follow.

- **Related**: [[science_discoveries#2178 — The Contact Signal]], [[history_timeline#2267 — The Kepler Anomaly|2267 Kepler Anomaly]]

## 2200s–2326 — The Mature Civilization

### 2201 — The Grand Council
Replaces Synthesis Council. Full planetary democracy — 1 vote per million citizens, including AI representatives. Earth's most sophisticated governance structure.

- **Related**: [[society_culture#What Happened to Nations]]

### 2234 — Quantum Communication at Scale
Faster-than-light communication achieved (not travel). Changes interplanetary civilization. Earth-Mars communication drops from 20-minute delay to instantaneous.

- **Related**: [[science_discoveries#2234 — Quantum Communication]]

### 2267 — The Kepler Anomaly
The Contact Signal decoded. Not alien — echo of our own 2178 broadcast, distorted by a temporal fold. Proved time is not strictly linear. Philosophical crisis follows.

- **Impact**: Launches temporal mechanics as a field
- **Related**: [[science_discoveries#2267 — The Kepler Anomaly]], [[technology#Time Research (Jake's Field)]]
- **Jake's connection**: This event directly inspired him to study temporal physics

### 2289 — Mars Independence
Mars declared independent sovereign nation. Earth-Mars War feared but averted through Ceres Accords.

- **Mars population at independence**: 420,000
- **First President**: Dr. Amara Vasquez (granddaughter of Elena Vasquez-Chen)
- **Related**: [[society_culture#Population and Demographics]]

### 2301 — Consciousness Backup Legalized
Human consciousness backup available (optional). Requires annual renewal. Most people choose not to use it — "death gives life meaning" movement strong.

### 2310 — Jake Born
Jakob Elias Morrow born in New Geneva. Parents: Dr. Iris Morrow (xenolinguist) and Prof. Thomas Morrow (20th century historian).

- **Related**: [[jake_personal]]

### 2326 — Jake Departs
Jake volunteers for temporal research mission to 2026. One-way travel. Youngest volunteer accepted by Temporal Research Initiative.

- **Related**: [[jake_personal#Basic Information]], [[technology#Time Research (Jake's Field)]]
