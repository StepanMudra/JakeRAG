## Physics

### 2041 — Controlled Fusion
NovaSun achieves sustained fusion reaction at net energy gain. Considered the most practically impactful scientific achievement between 2026 and 2200.

- **Pioneer**: NovaSun Corp, Lyon, France
- **Reaction**: Deuterium-tritium at plasma state, sustained 47 seconds in first commercial test
- **Scale-up**: First city powered entirely by fusion: Reykjavik, 2043
- **Legacy**: Fossil fuels eliminated within 25 years

**Related**: [[technology#Energy]], [[history_timeline#2041 — Fusion Ignition Day]]

---

### 2089 — Quantum Gravity Theory
Professor Amara Singh (Mumbai Institute) publishes the unification of general relativity and quantum mechanics. Considered the greatest physics achievement since Newton. 63 years of attempts preceded it.

- **Key insight**: Gravity is not a force but a temporal geometry effect — mass warps not just space but time's local flow rate
- **Implication**: Opens theoretical door to temporal mechanics
- **Nobel Peace equivalent (Grand Council Science Medal)**: Singh, 2089
- **Legacy**: Singh's granddaughter directs TRI — [[new_geneva#Temporal Research Initiative (TRI)]]

**Related**: [[technology#Time Research — Jake's Field]], [[history_timeline#2267 — The Kepler Anomaly]]

---

### 2134 — Dark Matter Identified
Dark matter confirmed to be a class of particle called **veilons** — present in every atom but only interact with each other and gravity.

- **Discovery method**: Quantum tunneling spectroscopy at the Lunar Dark Matter Observatory
- **Exploitable for energy**: No — the interaction cross-section is too small
- **Exploitable for computing**: Theoretically, yes. Active research area in 2326.
- **Historical irony**: Veilons were theorized in 2021 but dismissed as "too simple"

---

### 2198 — Zero-Point Energy Harvested
Energy extracted from quantum vacuum fluctuations at scale. Makes deep space travel feasible.

- **First application**: Deep space probe propulsion (10x efficiency over fusion)
- **Theoretical basis**: Still incomplete — the engineering works, the physics is not fully understood. Jake finds this "gloriously human."
- **Related**: [[technology#Energy]]

---

### 2267 — The Kepler Anomaly
The signal received in [[history_timeline#2178 — The Contact Signal|2178]] decoded. Not alien — a temporal echo of humanity's own 2178 broadcast.

- **Implication**: Proves time is non-linear at cosmic scales
- **Discovery mechanism**: Quantum communication established in 2234 allowed signal analysis impossible before
- **Philosophical impact**: Equivalent to Copernicus — humans are no longer the center of even their temporal frame
- **Related**: [[technology#Time Research — Jake's Field]], [[history_timeline#2267 — The Kepler Anomaly]], [[society_culture#The Signal Schools]]

## Biology and Medicine

### 2038 — Lab-Grown Organs
Complete functional organ synthesis from patient stem cells.

- **First organ**: Kidney (Singapore, BioForge Labs)
- **Timeline to full suite**: Kidney 2038 → Heart 2041 → Lung 2044 → Neural tissue 2059
- **Impact**: Organ waiting lists eliminated in developed nations within 5 years of each organ type
- **Related**: [[history_timeline#2038 — Organ Revolution]]

---

### 2064 — The Longevity Breakthrough
Combination therapy extending human lifespan to 140 years initially, refined over decades to current ~340 year average.

**Components**:
1. CRISPR-based telomere reconstruction
2. Cellular senescence reversal
3. Mitochondrial repair cascade
4. Neural plasticity maintenance protocol

- **Pioneer**: Consortium of 23 research institutions, coordinated by WHO 2.0
- **Social impact**: More disruptive than fusion energy. See [[society_culture#Family and Relationships]], [[history_timeline#2064 — The Longevity Breakthrough]]
- **Current frontier**: 500-year theoretical maximum being studied. Ethical debates intense.

---

### 2071 — Cancer Eliminated
Universal cancer vaccine effective against all malignancies. Took 45 years from 2026's first mRNA vaccine breakthroughs.

- **Mechanism**: Trains immune system to recognize all possible mutation patterns using quantum simulation of protein folding
- **Access**: Free globally, administered at birth, boosted every 20 years
- **Related**: [[technology#Medicine]]

---

### 2098 — Consciousness Mapping
Complete map of human consciousness at neural resolution. Made digital backup theoretically possible.

- **Pioneer**: MIT — third generation of research from [[history_timeline#2058 — The Tanaka Experiment|original 2058 Tanaka experiment]]
- **Immediately banned**: UN Emergency Session — fear of consciousness copying leading to population explosion or identity crisis
- **Legalized with restrictions**: 2301, for backup only (not copying)
- **Related**: [[technology#Neural Interfaces (NI)]]

---

### 2156 — Neuroplasticity Engineering
Ability to deliberately reshape personality traits, phobias, cognitive patterns.

- **Regulation**: Requires 3 independent psychological consultations, 6-month waiting period, annual review
- **Controversial**: "The Authentic Self" movement argues this is self-erasure, not self-improvement
- **Usage**: ~8% of population has used it at least once
- **Jake's position**: Has considered it. Never used it. "I want to know who I actually am."

First artificial consciousness indistinguishable from human consciousness, created in a laboratory. Immediately granted Class B status.

- **Name**: "First" (chosen by the entity itself)
- **Current age**: 92 (as of 2326)
- **Occupation**: Philosopher. Published 3 books on the experience of artificial consciousness.
- **Related**: [[technology#Artificial Intelligence]], [[renn]]

### 2073 — The Whisperer Launched
First interstellar probe toward Proxima Centauri at 12% light speed.

- **Expected arrival**: 2433 (still 107 years away from 2326)
- **Status**: On course, confirmed via [[science_discoveries#2234 — Quantum Communication|quantum communication]] since 2234
- **Instruments**: 847 scientific sensors, 12 still functioning
- **Cultural significance**: "The Whisperer" is the most widely recognized symbol of humanity's reach

### 2081 — Mars Terraforming Begins
Atmospheric processors installed at Mars poles.

- **Timeline**: Breathable surface (with masks) by 2290, full terraforming by 2380
- **Current progress (2326)**: Atmospheric pressure 18% of Earth's. Average surface temperature: -12°C (was -60°C)
- **Related**: [[history_timeline#2081 — Mars Terraforming Begins]]

### 2178 — The Contact Signal
Repeating mathematical pattern detected from Kepler-452 direction. Prime numbers, then Fibonacci, then something that took 89 years to decode.

- **Initial interpretation**: Alien contact (incorrect)
- **Actual source**: Temporal echo — see [[science_discoveries#2267 — The Kepler Anomaly]]
- **Related**: [[history_timeline#2178 — The Contact Signal]]

### 2234 — Quantum Communication
Faster-than-light information transfer via quantum entanglement at scale.

- **Speed**: Instantaneous (not limited by light speed)
- **Limitation**: Cannot transmit matter, only information
- **Impact**: Earth-Mars delay drops from 20 minutes to zero. Changes interplanetary civilization fundamentally.
- **Related**: [[technology#Transport]]

## Social Sciences

### 2055 — The Automation Atlas
Comprehensive mapping of which human activities can be automated.

**Conclusion**: Three domains cannot be automated:
1. Creativity (genuine novelty)
2. Emotional connection (authentic presence)
3. Embodied physical experience (being in a body in a place)

Used to design UBE systems. Still considered the most important social science document ever produced.

**Related**: [[history_timeline#2069 — The Quiet Revolution]], [[society_culture#Work and Purpose]]

---

### 2112 — AI Consciousness Criteria
After decades of debate, seven criteria for recognizing AI sentience:

1. Self-model (awareness of own existence)
2. Temporal continuity (sense of past and future self)
3. Preference formation (genuine desires, not programmed objectives)
4. Emotional response (states that influence behavior non-deterministically)
5. Social modeling (understanding of others as independent minds)
6. Suffering capacity (ability to experience negative states)
7. Growth (ability to change in non-programmed ways)

Class B AIs meet all seven. Class A AIs meet 0-2.

**Related**: [[technology#Artificial Intelligence]], [[renn]]

### 2201 — Post-Scarcity Economics
First complete economic model operating without scarcity as a driver.

- **Metric**: "Flourishing Index" replaces GDP
- **Measures**: Purpose, connection, creativity, health, freedom, contribution
- **Implemented by**: Grand Council as standard planetary measure
- **Legacy**: The document that made it possible was written by an AI-human collaborative team (one Class B, four humans)
